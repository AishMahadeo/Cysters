About CYSTERS

Welcome to CYSTERS! A platform for women to openly discuss PCOS. If you have any doubt in your mind whether you are prone to PCOS or not, take our test! Most of the time women are unable to recognize whether they have PCOS or not at its early stage and therefore it may get worse day by day. To eradicate this serious problem we built this model with motivation to provide a platform for women who feel shy about going to a doctor to check if they have pcos or not.
